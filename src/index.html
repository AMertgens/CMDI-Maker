CMDI Maker

About CMDI Maker

A tool for linguists for easily creating IMDI and CMDI metadata to upload to a language archive.


Part of CLASS - Cologne Language Archive Services
Developed by Sebastian Zimmer

For best results, use Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox with a viewport of at least 1280x720.
